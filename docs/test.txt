You can test the application by connecting to internet and typing a word in the search box.
The word can be typed in Latin, for example "mir".
The dictionary will convert the latin script into Cyrillic script and will display the meaning of the word (in the example above it is "peace" in Bulgarian language   )

Online Dictionary of the Bulgarian language for Android from "My Library" Chitanka.

Source of the dictionary - rechnik.chitanka.info.

Functions of the Bulgarian dictionary:

- Meaning of the word
- Synonyms
- Misspelling
- Random word
- Sharing the meaning of a word
